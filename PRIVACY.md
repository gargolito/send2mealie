# Privacy Policy

**Send to Mealie** respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
- **Mealie URL & API Key**: Stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.sync (encrypted by Chrome). Never transmitted to third parties.
- **Recipe URLs**: Only sent to YOUR Mealie instance when you click "Send to Mealie".
- **No tracking, analytics, or telemetry**.

## Permissions
- `storage`: To save your configuration locally
- `activeTab` + `scripting`: To inject the button on recipe sites
- `host_permissions`: Limited to whitelisted recipe domains

## Data Safety
All data is stored securely on your device. Your Mealie API key is never logged, cached, or shared.
